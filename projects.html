Projects

Prospection Patient Profile

Led a team of analysts to architect and build a modularised data processing pipeline and visualisation layer for US and Japan patient health insurance data. The product took patient data from various sources, cleaned it, analysed it, and presented patient insights in a dashboard. It was automated to run weekly when new patient data arrived.

Prospection NZ Data Ingestion

Worked with new patient data to identify relevant data fields for the business. Transformed that data and ingested it into the Prospection Platform. Designed and implemented a data update process around this automated ETL tool.

Services Australia Document Processing

Worked on a team designing and building a proof-of-concept document processing pipeline to identify, extract, and compartmentalise data from customer identification documents (passport, bank statement, etc.). OCR combined with NLP and computer vision. Docker containers deployed on-premises.

Phillip Island Nature Park Seal Spotter

Created a proof-of-concept computer vision application to count the number of seals in an aerial photograph of a seal colony. Convolutional Neural Network combined with thresholding and connected-component labelling.
